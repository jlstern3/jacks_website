Mission Statement

At Cedar & Crow our mission is to reconnect with our ancient ancestors and relearn the skills and practices that allowed them to survive and thrive in their time. By learning these skills, our aim is to make humans "wild" again. In this modern world we rely on so much modern technology that most of us have forgotten how proficient and capable we are in the natural world. By becoming what we like to call a "Wild Human" you will learn to be confident and proficient in the skills it takes to survive and thrive in the wilderness, as well as develop an appreciation for the natural world that very few people get to experience today.

Vision Statement

After attending every major naturalist school in the state of Washington, Jack Batteiger (Cedar & Crow Founder and Educator), has experienced the best of what these schools have to offer and identified ample room for improvements. Jack saw education getting watered down, and educational courses omitting important advanced skills and even essential basic skills. To combat the erasure of vital naturalist knowledge and wilderness skills, Cedar & Crow was born!
